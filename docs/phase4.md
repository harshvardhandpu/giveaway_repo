# Phase 4 - Investigation Reasoning

`InvestigationAgent` reconstructs ordered public-evidence timelines, computes source, extraction, relationship, recency, and uniqueness confidence, then flags public contradictions such as winner announcements that conflict with non-receipt statements. Its narrative is explainable decision support, not a claim of fact.

The API exposes investigation, timeline, and embedding-similarity cluster results. Similarity is a research lead requiring human review.
